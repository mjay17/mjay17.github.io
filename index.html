About this Visualization







Cost of Living Index

It is a relative indicator of consumer goods prices, including groceries, restaurants, transportation and utilities. It doesn't include accommodation expenses such as rent or mortgage.






Unemployment Rate

It tells us the percentage of the labor force that is unemployed.






Population Density

It is a measurement of population per unit area (in square miles).

Breakdown of Cost of Living Indexes

These indexes are relative to New York City (NYC). Which means that for New York City, each index should be 100(%). If another city has, for example, rent index of 120, it means that on an average in that city rents are 20% more expensive than in New York City. If a city has rent index of 70, that means on an average in that city rents are 30% less expensive than in New York City.

Groceries Index is an estimation of grocery prices in the city compared to NYC.

Restaurant Price Index is a comparison of prices of meals and drinks in restaurants and bars compared to NYC.

Rent Index is an estimation of prices of renting apartments in the city compared to NYC.

About this Visualization

Narrative Visualization Structure:
This narrative visualization follows an interactive slideshow hybrid structure. We walk viewer through a slideshow and in each slide there is an opportunity for viewer to explore details or skip to the next slide if they so desire.
This narrative walks viewer through global trends in life expectancy.

Scenes
I have used fullPage.js library for slide show effect. This library help me built a coherent narrative that can be easily tracked. Webpages are easily navigated either with a mouse scrolling or by clicking on specific page on the page navigation on the right.

Annotations
Various annotations are used in the pages:
The first page includes the overview and the key points of the narrative
In the following pages you can see annotations describing axes and titles
You can also see dynamic tooltips on the global life expectancy chart that provide additional information
A certain portion of the chart is also highlighted to draw viewer's attention to certain data point
Legend has also been provided on the charts

Triggers
You can hover your mouse over the global life expectancy chart for a tooltip to appear showing you the values for all the areas for that specific data point

Parameters
On the page showing healthy life expectancy vs total life expectancy a dropdown menu is a parameter that is used to filter the data based on the year.

Browser requirements
This website works with Chrome and Firefox. Please don't use Internet Explorer.

All data used in this project is available at the following websites:
